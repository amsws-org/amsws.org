to charitably further competency in the field of computer science for individuals who are new to the field Computing competency is a quickly growing need in all facets of industry, engineering, research, and government. Anyone and everyone, regardless of their background, should have the ability to work with computing devices easily and efficiently. The American Software Society is founded on the principle that everyone should have this opportunity. Our staff and volunteers are here to teach programming principles and skills to anyone new to computing. Whether they are students pursuing a degree in computer science, a professional looking to switch to a computing career, or someone looking to incorporate computing principles into their current career, students and clients of the American Software Society receive individualized instruction for their needs. The American Software Society is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization recognized as tax-exempt by the United States Internal Revenue Service. Your contributions are wholly tax-deductible and all amounts are highly valued and appreciated!
